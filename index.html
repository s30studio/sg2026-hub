Overview & Purpose

Studio Games 2026 is S30's flagship internal competition event, designed to foster healthy competition, showcase member progress, and bring the S30 community together across all four studios. This year marks a significant evolution: for the first time it runs as a true inter-studio competition with a Qualifier at each location followed by a cross-studio Finals.

Programming is aligned with Phase B, meaning members will have been actively preparing for these movements in the lead-up. All three workouts test across S30's core pillars: Strength, Conditioning, and Skill Performance.

2026 Key Evolution: Previous years were single-studio events. This year, Qualifier winners from each division advance to a cross-studio Finals. NSW studios compete at Surry Hills, QLD studios compete at Mermaid Beach.
Event Structure

Part 1: Qualifier

SAT 16 MAY · 8:00AM

Each studio runs their own internal Studio Games event. All 4 studios compete simultaneously at their own location. The top pair from each division at each studio qualifies for Finals.

Part 2: Finals · Studio vs Studio

SAT 23 MAY · 10:00AM

Division winners come together to compete Studio vs Studio. NSW Finals hosted at Surry Hills. QLD Finals hosted at Mermaid Beach. Best Friends session runs at host studios before the event.

Scoring Explained

Each division is ranked separately within each scored event. Points are awarded by finishing position. The pair with the highest total points across all four scores wins the division.

Score Workout What is measured Max Points
1A WOD 1 — STRONGER Combined Push Press kg (both athletes' best lift added together) — ranked highest to lowest 10
1B WOD 1 — STRONGER Combined Deadlift kg (both athletes' best lift added together) — ranked highest to lowest 10
2 WOD 2 — MAKE ME SWEAT Total combined calories at time cap — ranked highest to lowest 10
3 WOD 3 — SICKO MODE Total synchro kicksits in remaining time — ranked highest to lowest. Score = 0 if cap hit before 30 Hang Clean & Jerks. 10
Maximum Total Points 40
⚠️ Important: WOD 1 produces two separate scores (1A and 1B) — both are ranked and pointed independently. This means Strength accounts for 50% of the total score (20 of 40 possible points). This is intentional.

Qualification & Finals Format
NSW Finals: Surry Hills
  • Each division at Surry Hills + Edmondson Park produces 1 winning pair.
  • Up to 6 pairs per studio × 2 studios = up to 12 pairs at NSW Finals.
  • Same 3 workouts used at Finals. no new movements introduced.
  • Trophies/medals awarded to division winners.
QLD Finals: Mermaid Beach
  • Both QLD studios run Qualifier on Sat 16 May at their own locations.
  • QLD Finals hosted at Mermaid Beach on Sat 23 May.
  • Due to 2–3 hour travel distance, Maroochydore athletes attending Finals are not required to be division winners: any willing athletes can make the trip to represent their studio.

Staffing & Floor Setup

🎤 Head Judge

Briefs workouts, oversees day, settles disputes, announces results. Keeps energy up.

⚖️ Floor Judges

1 per 2 pairs on floor. Count reps, enforce standards, record scores.

📋 Score Recorder

Collects all scorecards, updates master leaderboard, flags disputes.

🔧 Setup Staff

2–3 staff: equipment transitions, athlete coordination, warm-up facilitation.

  • Max 4 pairs on floor at one time. Never mix Open + Pro in the same heat.
  • Zones: Competition Floor / Warm-Up Area / Spectator Area.
  • Setup complete by 7:30am. Staff briefing 7:30am. Doors open 7:45am.
  • Post heat order and schedule visibly on whiteboard or TV slide.
  • Finals day: Best Friends session ~8:00–9:30am. Floor competition-ready by 9:30am. Athlete doors open 9:45am.

Run all heats of one division before moving to the next. Max 4 pairs per heat at all times. Never mix Open + Pro. Use whiteboard to display current heat live.

Men's Open → Women's Open → Mixed Open → Men's Pro → Women's Pro → Mixed Pro

Heat Logistics
